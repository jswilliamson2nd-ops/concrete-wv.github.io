What is an ACI Interest Group?

An ACI Interest Group is a local, volunteer‑led community recognized by ACI that hosts technical sessions, networking, and outreach—often a stepping stone to an official ACI Chapter.

  • Connect practitioners, students, and agencies
  • Share best practices and new standards
  • Support certification and workforce development

Our near‑term mission

  • Build a contact list and steering committee
  • Plan a 2026 technical event series in WV
  • Coordinate with ACI on chapter requirements
